> Who I Am

I'm a dedicated cybersecurity student specializing in Digital Forensics and Information Security (DFIS) with hands-on experience in SOC operations, DFIR, vulnerability assessment, and Capture The Flag (CTF) competitions. Currently pursuing my Master's degree at Narnarayan Shastri Institute of Technology, affiliated with National Forensic Sciences University (NFSU), Gandhinagar.

Through multiple internships and practical training, I've gained experience across both offensive and defensive security disciplines. I'm passionate about incident response, forensic investigations, and continuous skill development through labs, certifications, and CTF competitions.
